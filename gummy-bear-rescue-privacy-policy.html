Privacy Policy

For the game: Gummy Bear Rescue

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data while you play our game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Gummy Bear Rescue, developed by Iron will as an Ad-Supported app. By using this app, you agree to the terms described here.

2. Information Collection and Use

In the event of an error in the app, certain data and information — referred to as Log Data — may be collected from your device through third-party services. This Log Data may include information such as:

• Device type and OS version
• IP (Internet Protocol) address
• App usage data (e.g., time spent in-game)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or location. All ads displayed in this app are non-personalized and filtered to be age-appropriate.

3. Third-Party Services

Gummy Bear Rescue uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

• Unity Analytics
• Unity Ads
• Google Play Services

These services may collect anonymized usage data for analytics, crash reporting, and serving non-personalized ads. Only Google Play certified ad networks are used, and all ads are filtered to be family-appropriate.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Ads in this app are non-personalized and appropriate for children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information.

5. Data Retention

Any data collected by third-party services (such as Unity or Google) is retained according to their respective policies. We do not store or retain personal data ourselves. Users (or parents/guardians of child users) may request deletion of data associated with the app by contacting us directly.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. User Rights (GDPR and COPPA Compliance)

Depending on your region, you may have the right to: Request access to data collected by third-party services. Request correction or deletion of your data. Restrict or object to certain types of data processing. Parents can exercise these rights on behalf of their children by contacting us.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page. Please check back periodically for updates.
